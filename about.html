RattleShedX

We are an innovation collective pioneering the integration of digital technologies and advanced manufacturing within the creative technology sector.

Our interdisciplinary team bridges the gap between cutting-edge technological development and artistic expression, creating novel solutions that push the boundaries of creative possibility.

Through close collaboration with institutions like Coleg y Cymoedd, we expand our service range and foster emerging talent, while ensuring a vital knowledge transfer back into the educational community.

Innovation Portfolio

Digital Design & Fabrication

Advanced 3D printing, rapid physical prototyping, digital twins and digital manufacturing processes tailored for creative applications.

Creative Technologies

Integration of AR/VR and interactive systems into artistic and entertainment applications. Modelling and manufactuing bespoke solutions for Creature FX models and props for games, film and TV.

Advanced Manufacturing

Industrial-grade 3D PolyJet printing, High-precision laser cutting and engraving, Professional 3D scanning and digitisation, Multi-material additive manufacturing, Digital fabrication workflows

R&D Innovation

Research into emerging technologies, materials and their practical applications within creative sectors. Knowledge exchange partnerships. Educational outreach

Consultancy

Providing advice on technology integration to SME and businesses. Running workshops and upskilling. Technology adoption strategies for traditional craft sectors

Product Design

We partner with artists and industry specialists to design and manufacture products that leverage their expert knowledge in such industries as music instrument care, jewellery and crafts.
